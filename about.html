About Us

The Backstory

Alex and Zach met in quite an unconventional manner: they met on GitHub when Zach commented on one of Alex’s pull requests. (I know, right? How nerdy is that!) However, Alex noticed that Zach was already working on a version of this project, and he asked if he could help with it. They talked a lot because they collaborated often, and after a while, Alex and Zach became good friends. Ultimately, Alex asked Zach if he would join Pandamonia.

Why the name?

Alex loves pandas. A lot. In fact, he has liked pandas ever since he and his sister encountered an animated GIF of a dancing panda online oh so many years ago. People say that his love for pandas can only be described with one word: pandamonium. The name Pandamonia comes from the non-existent plural form of this word which means “furor caused by or involving pandas” (Wiktionary).
