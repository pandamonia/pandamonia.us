Acey Deucey 2

Shuffle the Cards

Acey Deucey is a simple betting game in which two cards are drawn, and then the player bets if he or she thinks that a third card will be strictly between the first two cards. The further apart the two cards are, the better your chances.

Play Acey Deucey to unlock special achievements.
